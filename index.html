Established 1995

Thirty Years of
Precision Printing

Founded in 1995 by Sri Manjunatha, Manjunatha Offset Printers has been at the heart of Molakalmuru's commercial printing for over three decades. From a single press to a full-service print house, we have grown into one of the most trusted printing establishments in Chitradurga District.

We operate two professional offset machines — Ryobi 3200CD and Model 9920 — handling everything from precision wedding invitation runs to high-volume bill books, rubber stamps, and pre-ink stamps. Every order, large or small, gets the same professional treatment.
